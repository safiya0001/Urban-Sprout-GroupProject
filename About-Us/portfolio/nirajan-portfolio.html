About Me

A passionate developer building web applications and solving real-world problems through code. Currently exploring the vast world of front-end development and always eager to learn new technologies.

My Story

My interest in computers started in Grade 6 when I was first introduced to QBasic programming. It was the first time I understood how programs run and how computers follow instructions. In Grade 7, I learned HTML and created a simple website about the 2072 earthquake disaster in Nepal. Even though the design was not good, it taught me a lot and made me more curious about websites and technology.

During high school, I became more interested in computers, gadgets, cybersecurity, and how systems work. Exploring different tools and learning about online safety helped me understand the importance of problem-solving and continuous learning. These experiences made me enjoy programming because every project teaches something new and gives me a chance to create useful solutions.
