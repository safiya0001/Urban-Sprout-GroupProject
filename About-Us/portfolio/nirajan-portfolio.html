About Me

A passionate developer building web applications and solving real-world problems through code. Currently exploring the vast world of front-end development and always eager to learn new technologies.

My Story

My interest in computers started with simple curiosity. I was always drawn to exploring websites and applications, often wondering how everything worked behind the scenes. When I first learned programming, it felt challenging but exciting at the same time. Writing code and seeing immediate results—whether it was a small webpage or a simple feature—gave me a strong sense of satisfaction and confidence. It made me realize that technology is something I could create with, not just use.

As I continued learning, web development became more than just a skill—it became something I truly enjoyed. I liked the idea of building applications that people could interact with and benefit from. Each project taught me patience, problem-solving, and the value of continuous learning. This journey shaped my passion for web development and motivated me to keep growing in the field, both technically and creatively.
